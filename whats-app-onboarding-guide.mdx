Setting up the WhatsApp Business API allows you to scale your communications, automate support, and drive revenue. Because the API connects directly to Meta's infrastructure, the setup process requires strict adherence to Meta's security, identity, and billing protocols.

This guide walks you through the exact chronological steps to register your brand on Meseji, connect your WhatsApp number, and fully verify your business with Meta.

---

## Phase 1: The Pre-Flight Checklist

Before you begin, ensure you have the following assets ready on your desk.

<Warning>
  **Do not start the integration without these assets.** Incomplete setups or mismatched information can trigger automated account restrictions from Meta.
</Warning>

**Required assets:**

- **A Live Website & Domain Email:** For example, a live site at `www.yourbrand.com` and a matching professional email like `admin@yourbrand.com`.
- **Legal Documents:** A government-issued business registration (e.g., GST Certificate, Certificate of Incorporation) and a recent utility bill or bank statement (under 6 months old) matching your exact registered business address.
- **Facebook Admin Access:** Your personal Facebook login, which must grant you **Admin access** to your Meta Business Manager and your Facebook Page.
- **An Eligible Phone Number:** You must have a phone number capable of receiving a 6-digit OTP via SMS or Voice Call. _(Tip: If your number has an automated menu like "Press 1 for Sales," temporarily disable it so the Meta verification call can connect)._

### How do you want to use this phone number?

Depending on how you want to manage your chats, you must prepare your phone number using one of the two paths below **before** starting the setup:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Path A: The Dedicated API Number (Computer Only)" defaultOpen={false}>
    If you want to move this number entirely into Meseji to manage all customer chats from your dashboard, the number **cannot** be active on a mobile phone. You must open the standard WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app on your phone, go to **Settings \> Account \> Delete my account**, and delete it before proceeding.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Path B: WhatsApp Coexistence (Phone + Computer)" defaultOpen={false}>
    If you want the best of both worlds—meaning you keep using the WhatsApp Business app on your physical phone _while also_ using Meseji for bulk broadcasts and chatbots—you do **not** need to delete your app account. Simply open your phone's App Store or Google Play Store and ensure your WhatsApp Business app is updated to the latest version.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Phase 2: Meseji Platform Registration

Your first step is establishing your workspace within the Meseji platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Your Brand">
    1. Navigate to the signup page and create your account using your professional **domain email**.
    2. Enter your **Brand Name**. This should be the name your customers know you by.
    3. Verify your email address to activate the account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Organization Details">
    Once logged in, you need to configure your backend workspace.

    1. Navigate to **Settings \> Organization**.
    2. Enter your exact **Legal Business Name** and **Registered Address** _exactly_ as they appear on your tax documents.
    3. _(Optional)_ Invite your team members or support agents to the workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 3: The Meta Integration (Embedded Signup)

This is where you bridge your Meseji workspace with Meta's WhatsApp network.

### Step 3: Connect Meta WABA to Your Meseji Account

Once you have added your Organization details, navigate to the main **Dashboard** section inside Meseji and click the **Connect with Facebook** button. A secure Meta pop-up will appear.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Facebook">
    Use the personal profile that holds Admin rights to your Meta Business Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Portfolios">
    Choose your existing Meta Business Portfolio and Facebook Page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create WABA">
    Create a new WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Display Name">
    Enter your desired WhatsApp Display Name. It must closely match your legal name, website, or Facebook page, and cannot be in ALL CAPS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Phone Number">
    Enter your phone number and verify it via the 6-digit SMS or Voice Call OTP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Once the window closes, your number is successfully connected to Meseji in an unverified state. You can immediately send a limited number of test messages, but you must complete the next phase to scale your operations.
</Check>

## Phase 4: Meta Verification & Billing (Crucial for Scaling)

To lift messaging limits (beyond the trial tier), prevent account bans, and apply for the Green Tick, you must complete Meta's official verification and add a payment method for your messaging charges.

### Step 4: Complete Meta Business Verification

Meta must verify that you are a legally registered entity. This process happens entirely inside your Meta Business Manager, not on Meseji. You will need to upload your legal registration documents (like a GST certificate) and a proof of address.

<Card title="How to Complete Meta Business Verification" icon="shield-check" href="https://docs.meseji.app/meta-business-verification">
  Read our step-by-step guide on uploading documents and avoiding common rejection reasons to get your business verified fast.
</Card>

### Step 5: Add a Payment Method Directly to Meta

While you pay Meseji for the software platform, **Meta charges you directly for WhatsApp conversations** (e.g., Marketing, Utility, and Service messages). Before you can start broadcasting to your customers, you must add a valid credit card directly to your WhatsApp Business Account within the Meta billing portal.

<Card title="Setting up Meta Billing for WhatsApp API" icon="credit-card" href="https://docs.meseji.app/meta-billing-for-whats-app-api">
  Follow this tutorial to understand Meta's pricing tiers and see exactly where to add your credit card in the Meta Business Manager.
</Card>

## Phase 5: You're Live!

Once Step 4 (Verification) and Step 5 (Billing) are complete:

- Your messaging limits will automatically upgrade, allowing you to message thousands of customers.
- You can begin creating Meta-approved **Message Templates** for your marketing broadcasts inside Meseji.
- You are eligible to apply for the **Official Business Account (Green Tick)** to build ultimate trust with your audience.
