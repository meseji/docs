Meseji.app is a powerful communication platform designed to help you reach your customers where they are. Whether it's through **WhatsApp**, **SMS**, or **Email**, our AI-enabled tools ensure your messages are personalized, compliant, and high-converting.

## Core Capabilities

Our platform is built for developers and marketers who need reliability at scale.

  <Card title="WhatsApp Business API" icon="whatsapp">
    Official API integration for automated alerts, customer support, and marketing campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-Powered Context" icon="robot">
    Leverage AI to extract info from images (like visiting cards) and craft smart replies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Channel Routing" icon="split">

  </Card>

## Quick Start

Ready to dive in? Follow these steps to get your first campaign live.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up at [Meseji.app](https://meseji.app) and verify your business identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API Key">
    Navigate to the Dashboard settings to generate your unique API secret key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Channels">
    Connect your WhatsApp Business account or set up your SMS DLT templates (for India).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Use our [API Reference](/api-reference) to trigger your first outbound notification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Meseji?

In a crowded market, Meseji stands out by simplifying the complex world of Indian marketing regulations (like DLT) and adding a layer of AI intelligence to every interaction.

* **Low Latency:** Built on a modern tech stack (Next.js/NestJS) for instant message delivery.
* **Compliance Ready:** Built-in tools to manage opt-ins and Indian regulatory requirements.
* **Cost-Effective:** Zero-budget friendly scaling for startups and SMEs.
