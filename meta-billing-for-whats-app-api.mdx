# How to Add a Payment Method & Set Up Meta Billing

## Why You Must Set Up Billing Immediately

Even if you only plan to respond to customer inquiries (which are completely free), you still must have a payment method on file to send business-initiated templates (Marketing, Utility, Authentication).

If you attempt to send bulk templates without a verified card on file, or if your card fails, Meta will instantly block your outbound messages and your API logs will show **Error Code 131042 (Business eligibility payment issue)**. Furthermore, Meta will not upgrade your messaging tier limits if your billing account is not in good standing.

## Step-by-Step: Adding Your Payment Method

You handle all WhatsApp API billing directly within Meta's ecosystem.

### Step 1: Open Meta Business Settings

1. Log in to your [Meta Business Settings](https://business.facebook.com/settings).
2. Ensure you have selected the correct Business Portfolio from the top-left dropdown menu.

### Step 2: Navigate to Payment Settings

1. On the left-hand menu, scroll down to the **Accounts** section and click on **WhatsApp Accounts**.
2. Select the specific WhatsApp account you want to set up billing for.
3. Click on the **Settings** tab in the main window.
4. Click **Payment Settings** (this will redirect you to the "Billing & Payments" portal).

### Step 3: Add Your Business & Tax Information

_Before_ you add a card, you must provide your legal tax details. **Meta strictly enforces this in 2026.**

1. In the Billing portal, look for **Business Info** and click Edit.
2. Enter your Legal Business Name, Address, and **Tax ID** (e.g., EIN in the US, VAT in Europe, or GST in India). _Note: If you are in a region like India and fail to provide a GST number, Meta may reject your payment methods entirely._

### Step 4: Add Your Card

1. Click the **Add Payment Method** button.
2. **Set Your Region & Currency:** Select your Country and Currency carefully. _(Bonus: Meta now supports direct local billing in several global currencies, preventing you from paying foreign exchange bank markups!)_
3. **Select Method:** Choose **Credit or Debit Card** and click Next.
4. **Enter Details:** Input your card information. **Crucial:** Ensure the card has _International and Online Transactions_ explicitly enabled by your bank.
5. **Verify:** You may be redirected to your bank's 3D-secure page to enter an OTP or approve a micro-charge (usually refunded immediately) to authenticate the card.

## How Meta's Direct Pricing Works in 2026

If you used the WhatsApp API in the past, you might remember paying for "24-hour conversation sessions." As of the recent 2025/2026 updates, **Meta now charges strictly per delivered template message.**

Because we don't add any markups, you only pay Meta's official rate for the following categories:

- **Marketing Messages:** (Promotions, abandoned cart alerts, newsletters) These are your paid promotional messages. You are charged the base Meta rate for every marketing template delivered.
- **Authentication Messages:** (OTPs, login codes) Charged per delivered message at a lower rate than marketing.
- **Utility Messages:** (Order confirmations, shipping updates, payment receipts) These are significantly cheaper transactional messages.
- **Service Messages (100% Free):** When a customer messages you first, a 24-hour Customer Service Window opens. During this window, you can send unlimited free-form text replies at **absolute zero cost**. _(Bonus: Any Utility templates you send while this 24-hour service window is open are also completely free!)_

## Troubleshooting Error 131042: "Business Eligibility Payment Issue"

If your campaigns suddenly stop sending and your dashboard shows Error `131042`, Meta has restricted your account due to a billing snag. Here is how to fix it:

- **Missing Tax Info:** Double-check Step 3 above. If your region requires a Tax ID and you left it blank, Meta will block your API access.
- **Bank Blockades:** Banks frequently flag Meta's automated recurring charges as fraud. Call your bank to whitelist "Facebook/Meta Ads and WhatsApp Billing."
- **Timezone/Currency Mismatch:** Ensure the timezone and currency in your Meta ad/billing account perfectly match your registered business address.
