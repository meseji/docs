## Onboarding Process

Getting your official business profile live takes minutes. Follow these steps to begin sending messages at scale.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Meta Account Linking">
    Connect your Meta Business Portfolio to Meseji.app using our secure Embedded Signup flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Number Verification">
    Verify your dedicated business phone number via an OTP voice call or SMS from Meta.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Template Approval">
    Submit your first messaging template for approval to initiate outbound conversations.
  </Step>
